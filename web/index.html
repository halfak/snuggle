Snuggle Beta

Snuggle is an experimental user interface designed to aid in the identification of productive new Wikipedians. The system is still under heavy development, but your use of the system and feedback is welcome.

The system is developed and maintained by Aaron Halfaker. The original idea for the system came from his research into the cause of the decline of active editors in the English Wikipedia.
